NLS - IISC (Virtual) Workshop on AI and Law

2:30 PM - 6:30 PM, 14th December, 2021

The aim of the workshop is to explore research and education opportunities in AI and Law.
Participants: Invited Guests, IISc and NLSIU faculty

Session 1 : Educational Collabo​ration

A collaboration between IISc and NLSIU is in keeping with the mission statements of both universities and, as the NEP highlights, the need for a university system that is able to sustain multiple faculties, thereby offering students a multi-disciplinary education and encouraging cross-linkages in research. A coming together of the two universities allows for the creation of a liberal arts framework, where it will be possible for students to engage with the physical sciences and technology on one hand, and the humanities and social sciences, on the other.

Discussion : Educational Collaboration under the New Education Policy

Moderator : Prof. Raag Yadava (NLSIU)
2:40 PM - 3:25 PM

Session 2 : AI, Finance, and Law

This session focuses on the ways in which AI tools are being deployed across sectors specifically in the financial sector and their increasing role in decision-making processes - potential for leveraging their efficiencies and mitigating risks. The discussion will attempt to identify important and pressing research questions in this area.

Session 3 : Technologies to aid Justice

This session seeks to discuss the ways in which AI could be integrated into legal processes and systems to improve access to justice, remedy judicial backlogs and delays and build transparent, affordable and efficient legal systems. The panel will endeavour to highlight issues faced by courts and legal systems which would benefit the most from research collaboration.
